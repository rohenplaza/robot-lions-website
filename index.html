Team Journal

Robot Design Progress

August 28, 2024

Today we finalized the chassis design for our competition robot. The team decided on a 6-wheel drive system with pneumatic lifting mechanism. Our CAD models are complete and we're ready to begin construction.

Key Decisions:

  • 6-wheel drive for stability and maneuverability
  • Pneumatic lift system for efficient scoring
  • Lightweight aluminum frame construction

Programming Updates

August 25, 2024

Our programming team has been working on autonomous routines. We've successfully implemented a 15-second autonomous that scores 3 goals and parks in the zone.

Achievements:

  • Autonomous scoring routine completed
  • PID control for precise movements
  • Sensor integration for field positioning

Team Building Session

August 20, 2024

Had an amazing team building session today! We worked on communication skills and problem-solving exercises. The new members are integrating well with the team.

Looking forward to our first scrimmage next week!
